Pure strain humans (PSH) is the name given to Hominidae Homo Sapiens.
Pure strain humans are the collection of mammals control mundane terra. 
You are a pure strain human. 
The term pure strain refers to their lack of fun sciency fiction mutations or diseases of any sort.
Pure Strains can choose from any skin colour: black, blue, white, green, yellow, red, gold, etc.
Pure Strains possess none of the mythical strengths or weaknesses from unpopular culture.

There is a small chance a pure strain may catch a psionic mutation.
If the player does not want her persona to mutate, a pure strain human persona will not mutate.
If the persona does mutate, she cannot have a defect or a mutation that alters her appearance.
