== Points to Ponder on Alien Combat

. Aliens represent entire species.
. Flora and Fauna rarely have tactics.
. Flora and Fauna see technology as terrain, like sticks and rocks.
. Only tool using aliens use weapons.
.. Weapon using aliens use a vocation combat table.
. Naturally evolved attacks use Bonus Proficient on the combat table
. Aliens do not have damage adjustment for their natural attacks.
.. Aliens do have damage adjustment when they use weapons.
. Only tool using aliens use armour.
.. Alien naked armour rating is randomly determined.
.. Descriptive body part types do not affect armour rating.
. Alien die at zero hit points.
.. Aliens do not suffer damage system shock and can fight until dead.
