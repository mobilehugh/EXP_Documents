A maintenance robot is a mechanic in a drum.
This robot acts as a functioning xref:vocations:mechanic.adoc[mechanic,window=_blank].
"Maint" robots are interested in maintaining, repairing and improving inorganic objects.

The maintenance robot gets more bonuses and skills than a typical mechanic.
The player adds two times her Intelligence score to any maneuver rolls.
The maint robot starts with many more skills than a regular mechanic.
The player gets one roll on the mechanic skill table per two points of Intelligence score.
