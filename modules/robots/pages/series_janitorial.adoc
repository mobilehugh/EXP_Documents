= Janitorial

Janitorial robots clean up.
Their philosophical goal is to decrease the entropy of the universe.
Janitorial robots have vacuums, sweepers, cleaners, plungers etc.
If the persona's Intelligence score is 12 or better the player may choose to be a domestic cleaner.
Domestic janitors are smarter due to the rapidly shifting domestic environment.
Commercial janitors tend to work in more regimented and dangerous environs.
