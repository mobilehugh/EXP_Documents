== Estimating Persona Hite from Wate
If the entirely random hite and wate does not work for your persona consider the below (now deprecated) table.
The Persona Wate and Dimensions Table underestimates the hite of the robot or alien. 

NOTE: Do not let rules get in the way of the story or fun.

[discrete]
== feeling dense?
Wate to hite estimates are based on the https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Specific_weight[following,window=_blank, rel="noopener noreferrer"]

. 1 tonne of dense electronics/meat occupies 3.5 cubic meters
. 3.5 cubic meters is 1.2 x 1.2 x 2.4 meters of volume
. very roughly 1 tonne of persona is 1 hex of hite
. your plot and usage may vary
