= Toy Pre Rolls

Pre rolled so you don't have to.

The following table acts as a catalogue and generator or pre rolled toys.
Roll a 1d1000 for an anything goes pre roll.
Keep in mind that the Raw Roll could generate anything from a deflated soccer ball to a military spacecraft. 
If you are looking for something from a particular department grab use a 1d100 to get the pre roll of your choice.
If you are looking for something specific see if it in the catalogue list of all the pre rolls.

.*How to use the catalogue*
* Left Column: Raw roll for anything (1d1000)
* Center Column: Catalogue for shopping
* Right Column: Departmental roll limited to department (1d100)
